How is an electricity bill calculated in India?

Most Indian domestic electricity bills use telescopic slabs. The first block of units is charged at the first rate, the next block at the next rate, and so on. Fixed charge, meter rent, electricity duty, and monthly fuel adjustment charges are then added.
