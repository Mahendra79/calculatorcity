Area Converter

The area converter handles both international units (sq m, sq ft, acre, hectare) and Indian land measurement units (cent, ground, guntha, bigha) that are used across different states for property transactions. If you are buying land in Andhra Pradesh or Tamil Nadu, you will encounter cents and grounds. If you are in Maharashtra or UP, guntha and bigha are common. This converter supports all of them.

How to Use

  1. Type the number you want to convert in the Value field at the top.
  2. Select the unit you are converting from in the left dropdown.
  3. Select the unit you want to convert to in the right dropdown.
  4. Read the live result below; there is no calculate button.
  5. Use the Swap button to reverse the conversion direction instantly.

FAQ

What is a cent in land measurement and where is it used?

A cent is 1/100 of an acre, equal to about 40.4686 m² or 435.6 sq ft. It is common in South India.

How many square feet are in one acre?

One acre equals 43,560 square feet.

What is a ground in Tamil Nadu property measurement?

One ground is 2400 sq ft, approximately 222.967 m².

How does bigha differ between states in India?

Bigha is a traditional land unit and its size changes by region. Always confirm the local definition before a property transaction.

How do I convert square metres to square feet?

Multiply square metres by 10.7639 to get square feet.
